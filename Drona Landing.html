— 11 / Help

FAQ

The questions students, parents and teachers ask us most often.

What makes DRONA different from ChatGPT or Gemini?+

DRONA is not one model — it's a faculty. A Head Agent orchestrates specialised subject agents, a Socratic agent, a Stats agent, a Scheduler and a Quest agent. They share a vector memory-mesh of every interaction you've ever had. ChatGPT forgets you. DRONA refuses to.

Is the free plan really free?+

Yes. We route the Seeker plan through Gemini Flash, Groq Mixtral, OpenRouter free pools and Ollama local fallback. You get the Head Agent and one subject agent, 100 messages/day, and the basic Stats radar — forever, with no credit card.

Can I bring my own API key?+

Absolutely. The BYOK Engine wraps your GPT-4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet or Gemini Pro key in our master Teacher Persona prompts. You pay raw compute to OpenAI/Anthropic; we charge ₹199/month for the orchestration.

How does the assessment work?+

An Assessment Agent conducts a personalised interview and offers an optional 100-mark, 90-minute test (75 MCQ + 25 written). A Grader Agent ruthlessly evaluates and a Profile Agent compiles your strengths/weaknesses. Skip it and the system will profile you live during your first 3-5 sessions.

Are my notes and chats private?+

Yes. Memory-mesh runs in your private vector namespace. We never train base models on your data. You can export or wipe everything at any time from Workspace → Settings.

When will Boss-Battle Arenas and Marketplace go live?+

Arenas launch in Phase 1 with the MVP. The physical Marketplace (MacBooks, books, stationery for Top-100 yearly leaderboard) activates once the platform crosses a defined active-user threshold.
