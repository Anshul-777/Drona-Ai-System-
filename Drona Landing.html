FAQ

Questions learners and parents ask most often.

What makes DRONA different from generic learning tools?+

DRONA is built as a complete learning experience—structured teaching, environment-based practice, and progress that carries forward across your entire journey.

Is the free experience really usable?+

Yes—Seeker is designed for genuine practice. You get core learning flow and progress visibility without pressure or setup complexity.

Can I customize how the learning feels?+

Yes. DRONA supports different coaching styles and lesson depth preferences so the experience matches the way you learn.

How does the test experience help me?+

After every mock, you receive targeted breakdown insights and next-step practice guidance—so attempts become smarter and outcomes improve.

Is my learning private?+

DRONA is designed with privacy in mind. Your learning experience stays under your control, and you can manage what’s stored in your learning workspace.
